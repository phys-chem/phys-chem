DFT, MD and ML researcher

  • My research experience include three parts, density functional theory (DFT), molecular dynamics (MD), and machine learning (ML). I apply DFT to study the interaction and reaction mechanisms of novel systems such as cylco[18]carbon. In addition, I am experienced in multiscale MD from ab initio to machine learning potential, molecular force fields, and coarse-grained simulations. Finally, I also work to construct new descriptors for predicting the properties of molecules and materials based on the Δ-ML method.
